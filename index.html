Explore hand-crafted scenes, uncover secrets, and train your eye. Mystery Tour: Hidden Object is a relaxed yet focused puzzle experience: search each location for the items on your list before the clock runs out. New scenes keep the challenge fresh—speed, focus, and attention to detail are what separate a quick glance from a true find.

The journey

Every level drops you into a richly detailed environment. Spot objects hidden in plain sight, work through time-based goals, and progress through a variety of settings. Whether you have a few minutes or a longer session, the loop stays simple to pick up and satisfying to clear.

What you will find

  • Immersive hidden-object play: Scenes designed for close looking and list-based searching.
  • Time challenges: Clear stages against the timer for an extra layer of pressure.
  • Polished presentation: High-detail artwork and a UI built for quick reads on small screens.
  • Multiple locations: Roam different environments as you advance.
  • Game Center: Leaderboards for friendly competition (where available).
